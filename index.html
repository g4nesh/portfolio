Ganesh Talluri

Hi! I'm Ganesh Talluri, a student at BASIS Peoria based in Peoria, AZ. I am the co-founder of Luxen LLC and a machine learning researcher.

My work spans autonomous AI agents, medical imaging segmentation, and computational biology. I am passionate about building trustworthy AI systems and applying deep learning to solve complex biological problems. I have also worked on fine-tuning deep learning models for out-of-distribution tasks and created generalizable models for image analysis. Additionally, I have worked on parameter efficient methods for creating low-latency computer use agents using frontier models.

Most recently, I was a Ventures-TECH Winter Fellow at Harvard University and a Don Lavoie Fellow at George Mason University. I am currently an Associate Scholar at theMasason Foundation and a Research Assistant at Midwestern University, advised by Prof. Andrew Lee.

Feel free to reach out if you are interested in my work!
